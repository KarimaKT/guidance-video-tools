# Portable skill: using Guidance Video Tools

Use this guidance when operating the app.

## Meeting sanitizer workflow

1. Start with the recording and transcript.
2. Run an audit before rendering.
3. Check detected speaker names exactly as they appear in the transcript.
4. Review every proposed cut and keep range.
5. Add manual overrides for anything the AI should keep or remove.
6. Verify representative frames for masks before final export.
7. Render a preview before publishing.

## What to inspect before sharing

- The opening and ending lines are complete.
- Presenter names are preserved when they should be visible.
- Non-presenting participants are masked.
- Both people are visible only when the section is conversational.
- Clips have clean audio and are not corrupted.
- Title and ending screens are sanitized and do not expose private meeting details.

## Demo/test gallery

Use the Test Gallery to compare current outputs against curated examples. Keep the gallery focused on current app demos, generated guided web tours, learning-series videos, and the approved webinar clips; do not show old demo clips or stale samples as if they are current product output.

## Demo Video Creator

Use Demo Video Creator for short app/product demos and guided web tours.

1. Start with the product name, audience, and value before mechanics.
2. Use slides for context, framing, and closing takeaways.
3. Use generated web tours or uploaded video clips as embedded video scenes.
4. Keep guided web-tour screen beats short and visibly marked with step/time context.
5. Add narration throughout; do not leave a silent walkthrough in the middle of a demo unless silence is intentional and reviewed.
6. Download the sample YAML before uploading your own script if you need the expected fields.

## Technical Explainer Studio

Use Technical Explainer Studio when the goal is teaching, not just showing. It is for source-grounded topics, misconceptions, mental models, learning series, and deeper explanation.
